WhatsApp Chatbot Flow – Region wise 
Whatsapp will automatically detect the person’s country/region from the sender’s number:
* Pakistan (+92)
* India (+91)
* Bangladesh (+880)
* UAE (+971)
* Saudi (+966)
* Etc


Based on the region a dynamic menu will be displayed to the person as follow;
* Pakistan: English, Urdu, Roman Urdu
* India: English, Hindi, Urdu, Roman Urdu
* Bangladesh: English, Urdu, Roman Urdu, Bangla
* Middle East: English, Urdu, Arabic, Roman Urdu
* Other Countries (agar country kisi aur region ki ho uper diay gy regions k ilawa): English, Urdu, Roman Urdu
